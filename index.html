About Steve Smith

Steve Smith is a world-class Australian cricketer known for his unorthodox batting technique, mental toughness, and consistency. Originally starting as a leg-spinner, he transformed into one of the finest Test batsmen of all time. Smith has also captained Australia and is admired for his resilience and leadership.
